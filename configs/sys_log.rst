========
SysLog
========

SysLog处理器配置
================

+---------------+--------------+---------+------------------------------------+
|参数名         |说明          |是否可选 |默认值                              |
+===============+==============+=========+====================================+
|ident          |标识          |是       |false                               |
+---------------+--------------+---------+------------------------------------+
|option         |选项          |是       |LOG_CONS \| LOG_NDELAY \| LOG_PID   |
+---------------+--------------+---------+------------------------------------+
|facility       |级别          |是       |LOG_USER                            |
+---------------+--------------+---------+------------------------------------+

.. note::

   SysLog 的参数请参考 `openlog() <https://www.php.net/manual/zh/function.openlog.php>`_ 函数。
